# Your Life in Weeks

A 90-year life is just 4,680 weeks.

This is a single-page, zero-dependency website that visualizes your entire 
life as a grid of dots — one dot per week. Enter your birthdate and instantly 
see how many weeks you've lived, the one you are in right now, and all the 
ones still ahead.

## Live Demo
yourusername.github.io/life-in-weeks

## How to Use
1. Open the website
2. Enter your date of birth
3. Hit Reveal
4. Share it with someone you love.

## What You'll See
- Filled dots — weeks already lived
- Gold dot — the week you are living right now
- Empty dots — weeks still ahead

## Features
- Fully personal — built around your exact birthdate
- Shareable URL — your grid is encoded in the link
- Stats: weeks lived, weeks remaining, summers left, Mondays ahead
- 100% local — no data is ever stored or sent anywhere

## Inspiration
Based on Tim Urban's essay "Your Life in Weeks" from Wait But Why.
